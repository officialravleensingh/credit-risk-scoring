##  Tips for Success

1. **Practice your section 3-4 times** before recording
2. **Speak clearly and at moderate pace** - not too fast
3. **Keep slides visible** for at least 5 seconds each
4. **Pause between major points** - helps with editing
5. **Don't worry about perfection** - 2-3 takes is normal
6. **Stay hydrated** - keep water nearby
7. **Have fun!** - Your enthusiasm will show

##  Troubleshooting

### Issue: Slide not visible
**Solution:** Ensure full screen mode, check resolution

### Issue: Audio too quiet
**Solution:** Move closer to mic, increase input volume

### Issue: Background noise
**Solution:** Close windows, turn off fans/AC, use quiet room

### Issue: Timing too long/short
**Solution:** Adjust speaking pace, trim pauses in editing

### Issue: Demo not working
**Solution:** Test beforehand, have backup recording ready
