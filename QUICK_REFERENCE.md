## 🎙️ RECORDING TIPS

1. **Speak clearly** - Not too fast, enunciate
2. **Pause between slides** - 1 second minimum
3. **Keep slides visible** - At least 5 seconds each
4. **Match script timing** - Practice beforehand
5. **Quiet environment** - Close windows, turn off fans
6. **Good lighting** - Face should be visible if on camera
7. **Test audio** - Record 10 seconds, play back
8. **Backup recording** - Use phone as backup
